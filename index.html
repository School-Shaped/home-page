Teachers, Engineers, and Designers

Together, let's create the tools and experiences our schools deserve.

Let's build a community

School Shaped enables change in the classroom by partnering teachers with teams of engineers and designers to create amazing student experiences. For free.

How does it work?

A School Shaped team of designers and engineers observe a classroom, and learn from a teacher

The teacher and the team work together to design and prototype a tool or experience the teacher wants

The teacher get's free use of the prototype, and the team documents the results
