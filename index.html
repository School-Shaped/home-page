Teachers, Engineers, and Designers

Together, let's create the tools and experiences our schools deserve.

How does it work?

Teachers

You want tools and experiences in your classroom, but don't have the time or resources to make them.

Builders

You want to make great tools and experiences for schools, but don't know where to start.

School Shaped

 

Learn

A School Shaped team of designers and engineers observe a teacher at work, to discover what it means to teach and learn in today's classroom.

Create

The teacher and the team work together to design, prototype and test a tool or experience the teacher wants

Inspire

The process and results are documented online, to inform and inspire other developers as they create the next generation of edtech.

All for free

Meaningful Results

Teacher receive the tools and experieces they want, but don't have time to make themselves. This allows them to focus on the thing that matters most: creating a great experience for their students.

School Shaped teams gain insight into a classroom, professional mentorship and experience. They leave the program ready to create amazing edtech.

We empower our partner communities to imagine new uses of the classroom through design thinking, even after the program is done.
