School Shaped

Software built with schools, for schools.

What we do

School Shaped is like an year long startup incubator where small teams, design, build and distribute software as part of a school community.

We mentor "Build Teams" as they work with students, teachers and administrators to design and prototype new tools.

Effective prototypes are documented and distributed through our trusted App Platform.

Following the year, we help our Build Teams develop their ideas into a startup, funded by partner incubators and investors.
