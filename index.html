School Shaped

Software built with schools, for schools.

School Shaped is a year long incubator where small teams join a school community to design, build, and share software.

We mentor "Build Teams" as they design and prototype with students, teachers and administrators.

We document each prototype and distribute it through our trusted App Platform.

We help Build Team graduates turn their ideas into businesses, connecting them with partner incubators and investors.
