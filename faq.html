FAQ

What kind of support does an adovocate usually provide?

Nearly anything that a teacher requires to innovate in their classroom. Advocates may connect a teacher with another educator in the community, for advice. They may build software for the teacher, or finding others who can build software. They may just do research, to help inform a new project the teacher is wants to try out. Through an advocate and the community they work with, a partner teacher can receive a wide range of supports.

What kind of schools do you normally work with?

We like to work with low income schools when possible - they have the most need for extra resources. However, our most important criteria is having a partner teacher that is excited to work with us, regardless of school type!
